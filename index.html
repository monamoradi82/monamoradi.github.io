About Me

I am an AI Engineer specializing in Machine Learning, Edge AI, and IoT for digital healthcare. My work focuses on developing real-time, on-device intelligence for medical applications, improving latency, security, and scalability across distributed healthcare environments.

With over 10 years of experience in academia and industry, I bring expertise in computer engineering, computer architecture, and hardware design, including VLSI and nanoscale technologies. I enjoy working at the edge of emerging technologies, bridging advanced research with real-world applications.

Beyond research, I enjoy exploring new places and cultures. I am particularly fond of music, cooking, books, and new experiences. I am also an avid reader of historical and philosophical literature, continuously seeking new perspectives and insights.
